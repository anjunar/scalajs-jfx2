Interaction
A button is small, but it carries responsibility.
In JFX2 the action stays visible in the template: label, event, and surrounding context sit next to each other. That keeps simple buttons easy to read and leaves room for more complex workflows later.
Standard button
A focused click target with direct event binding. Ideal for clear, self-contained actions.
Buttons are the heart of interaction. They are not just click targets; they bring your app to life.
Action group
Several buttons may sit close together as long as their intent remains distinguishable.
